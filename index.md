# Azaan — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 23 July 2026**

Azaan is a prayer-times, azan-reminder, and qibla-compass app. It is built on a
simple principle: **we do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any of
your data. Ever.**

## Location
Azaan uses your device's location solely to calculate prayer times and the
qibla direction. This calculation happens **entirely on your device**. Your
location is never sent to us or to anyone else — the app makes no network
requests with your location.

## Data collection
- No accounts. No sign-up.
- No analytics, no trackers, no advertising SDKs.
- No personal data is collected or transmitted.
- Notifications are scheduled locally on your device.
- If you choose a custom azan sound, the app only reads the audio file you
  picked; it is not copied or uploaded anywhere.

## Third parties
Azaan contains no third-party advertising or analytics services. Prayer times
are computed offline using the open-source Adhan library.

## Children
Azaan does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Any change to this policy will be published at this page.
